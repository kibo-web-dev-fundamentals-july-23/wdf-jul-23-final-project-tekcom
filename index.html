  • Create a Zoom Account

    1. After setting up your Kibo Google Account, create a Zoom account using your Kibo email. Use the Sign in with Google option and select your Kibo account.

    2. After creating your Zoom account:

    3. Download the Zoom desktop client here. Open the desktop app and sign in with your Kibo account
    4. Go to the zoom web app (https://zoom.us/), click "Sign In" at the top right, and sign in with your Kibo account

    5. Download the Mentimeter Zoom App
